Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our catering services

How far in advance should I book catering services?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for smaller events and 2-3 months for weddings or large corporate events. However, we do accommodate last-minute requests when possible.

Do you provide serving staff and equipment?

Yes, we offer full-service catering which includes professional serving staff, appropriate serving equipment, and cleanup. These services can be customized based on your event needs and budget.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We're experienced in catering to various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and other specific requirements. Please mention any dietary restrictions in your request form.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 7-14 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 7 days of the event are non-refundable.

Do you offer tastings before booking?

Yes, we offer tasting sessions for wedding packages and large corporate events. There is a small fee for tastings which is deducted from your final bill if you proceed with booking our services.
